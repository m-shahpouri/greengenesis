Information About us

Green genesis is a diverse General Contractor firm that specializes in various engineering services for clients in international markets. Green genesis caters to customer needs by providing a comprehensive range of project services, starting from process design and extending to equipment procurement, plant construction, commissioning, and operator training.

Established in May 1988, Green genesis boasts a team of experienced and accredited professionals who have successfully completed numerous projects within their respective areas of expertise.

We are dedicated to delivering innovative and all-encompassing solutions to significant water-related challenges. Green genesis actively participates in projects within the Energy, Water-Environment, and infrastructural sectors. Our esteemed clients include oil companies, urban operators, governments, municipal corporations, international project developers, insurance companies, and industrial zone companies.

With a workforce of over 2,000 employees, including our partnerships with other companies, Green genesis operates in more than six countries. Our versatile fleet comprises of over 1,000 machinery and equipment.

The demand for project services is driven by several global trends, including the increasing energy consumption and the pursuit of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the growth in global trade and competitive markets, the rise in world population and the need for improved social services, as well as the impact of climate change and the associated water and environmental crises.

Green genesis is well-equipped to address these trends with a wide range of high-quality services, which positions us favorably for balanced growth and promising prospects.

With over 30 years of experience, Green genesis has developed expertise in various fields, including Process Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Offshore and Onshore Engineering, Hydraulics, Hydrology, Structural, Electrical, Mechanical, Instrumentation, and SCADA.

Recently, Green genesis has embarked on a significant endeavor to enhance and restructure its operations into the General Contractor model, aligning with a long-term strategic plan.
