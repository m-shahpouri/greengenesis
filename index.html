Information About us

Green genisis is a multi-disciplinary General Contractor firm, offering a broad range of engineering services to clients in international markets. Green genisis provides a full range of project services tailored to suit customer needs, from process design through to equipment procurement, plant construction, commissioning and operator training.

Founded in May of 1988, Green genisis's experienced and accredited professionals have successfully completed references within its areas of practice

We deliver innovative one-stop solutions to major water challenges. Green genisis is active in projects in the Energy, Water-Environment and infra markets. Our main clients include oil companies, Urban operators, Governments, Municipalities companies, International project developers, Insurance companies and Industrial Zone companies.

Green genisis has more than 2,000 employees, including our share in partner companies. We operate in over 6 countries. Our versatile fleet consists of over 1000 machinery and equipment.

Demand for project services is driven by a number of global trends:

  • Growing energy consumption and deserving Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
  • Growth in global trade, Rivalry and competitive market
  • Growth in world population and required social services for better living
  • Climate change and water and environment crisis

Green genisis has a wide range of high-quality services to deal with these trends, giving us excellent prospects for balanced growth.
During over 30 years of experience, Green genisis Expertise is gained in Process Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Offshore and Onshore Engineering, Hydraulics, Hydrology, Structural, Electrical, Mechanical, Instrumentation, and SCADA.

Green genisis has recently launched a major effort to upgrade and modify its structure into the General Contractor pattern based on the long-term strategic plan.
