Information About us

Green genesis is a diverse General Contractor firm that specializes in various engineering services for clients in international markets. Green genesis caters to customer needs by providing a comprehensive range of project services, starting from process design and extending to equipment procurement, plant construction, commissioning, and operator training.

Established in May 1988, Green genesis boasts a team of experienced and accredited professionals who have successfully completed numerous projects within their respective areas of expertise.

We are dedicated to delivering innovative and all-encompassing solutions to significant water-related challenges. Green genesis actively participates in projects within the Energy, Water-Environment, and infrastructural sectors. Our esteemed clients include oil companies, urban operators, governments, municipal corporations, international project developers, insurance companies, and industrial zone companies.

With a workforce of over 2,000 employees, including our partnerships with other companies, Green genesis operates in more than six countries. Our versatile fleet comprises of over 1,000 machinery and equipment.

The demand for project services is driven by several global trends, including the increasing energy consumption and the pursuit of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), the growth in global trade and competitive markets, the rise in world population and the need for improved social services, as well as the impact of climate change and the associated water and environmental crises.

Green genesis is well-equipped to address these trends with a wide range of high-quality services, which positions us favorably for balanced growth and promising prospects.

With over 30 years of experience, Green genesis has developed expertise in various fields, including Process Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Offshore and Onshore Engineering, Hydraulics, Hydrology, Structural, Electrical, Mechanical, Instrumentation, and SCADA.

Recently, Green genesis has embarked on a significant endeavor to enhance and restructure its operations into the General Contractor model, aligning with a long-term strategic plan.

Iran

Jahan Foulad Sirjan factories

In August 2023, Jahan Foulad Sirjan Co. partnered with Green genesis Co. to provide purified water for its industries, including the No. 1 smelting and casting factory. The 720 m3/hr capacity RO water purification system includes sand and active carbon filters, RO unit's skids, and ancillary equipment.

2023 Jul - 2024 Jun

Enhancing Waste Water Treatment in South Pars Gas Plants

South Pars gas field, located on Iran-Qatar border, requires wastewater treatment upgrades. Pars Oil and Gas Co. signed a contract with Green genesis Co. to improve treatment units. Site one will have a centralized plant for 8 refineries, while site two will have three treatment plants for remaining refineries.

2023 Feb - 2024 Jan

Shadegan Steel Complex

Shadgan steel, a significant national project, operates with 800k tons annual steel ingot production. Second phase development is planned with equal capacity. Green genesis Co. signed a contract for water treatment and cooling tower systems installation and operation. Project includes RO equipment, WTP cooling water, cranes, and piping.

2022 Jul - 2023 Nov

Bahabad Pelletizing Project

Pars Land Industries and Mines Construction Company (Pamidco) contracted Green genesis Co. for a Sanitary wastewater treatment package in Chadormelo factory. Capacity is 60 m3/day. Process units include balancing tank, garbage collection, biological tanks, sedimentation tank, chlorination, and filtration unit. Sludge is stored and sent to depot.

2022 Feb - 2024 Mar

Taleqan dam water supply to Qazvin

Green genesis Co. and Jahad-Nasr-Kerman joint venture awarded contract for Taleqan dam water supply transfer line to Qazvin. Scope includes procurement and execution of steel pipe water transfer line, valve rooms, concrete storage tanks, and steel bridges.

2017 Jul - 2020 Jul

DBO for rural wastewater systems, Zone 2

The contract involves designing, building, and operating wastewater collection and treatment systems for 29 villages in the center part of Iran, Zone 2. The scope of work includes executing sewerage systems for 186 villages, operation and maintenance of all facilities, and constructing wastewater treatment plants, pump stations, and transmission lines.

2017 Feb - 2020 Feb

Construction of irrigation network of Garmsiri pump station second complex

The contract entails engineering, procurement, construction, installation & operation of a pump station for a tropical water regeneration project in Kermanshah Province, aiming to manage surface water, prevent floods, provide agricultural water, and meet urgent needs of the province's people.

2016 Oct - 2021 Oct

Chitgar Lake water treatment plant

Contract involves the construction of a treatment plant at Chitgar Lake in Tehran, Iran. The project aims to improve the quality of water entering the lake and includes the design and build of water treatment plants, with an inflow discharge of 1200-2400 Lit/h. Green genesis Co. is responsible for the operation of the plant for 24 months.
